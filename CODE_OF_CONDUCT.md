# Code of Conduct

PROMPTRON is a friendly open-source project. Be respectful, constructive, and patient with people of all skill levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming language.
- Give feedback that helps the work improve.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Publishing private information.
- Repeated disruption of conversations or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who make the project hostile or unsafe. Reports can be sent privately to the maintainer through the public profile links in the README.
